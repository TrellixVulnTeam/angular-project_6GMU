Who Are We

The company was built in the twenty-first century due to the spread of cosmetics so much that it became an essential thing that women cannot do without, and thousands of cosmetics used in all areas of cosmetics and for both sexes spread, and there are beauty centers specialized in this field. New techniques and trends for makeup have also spread, such as "contour", which is three-dimensional makeup, the aim of which is to sculpt and illuminate the face so that defects are hidden and the aesthetics of the face are highlighted, by reducing areas and highlighting other areas of the face. And the baking technique that depends on the powder, where a dense layer of free powder is placed by a small sponge under the eye, cheekbones, along the nose and on the chin, and the powder should be left on the face for a period ranging from 5 to 20 minutes, so that the powder gradually melts with the skin by heat The body, thus increasing its ability to cover, lasts longer and gives the skin a brighter appearance. The term "Make-up Artist", which means a makeup artist, appeared globally, and in turn spread in the Arab world, and many Arab make-up experts appeared through social media programs to cross the borders of the Arab world and gain fame from all over the world.
